ABOUT ASHLEY

Regional Australia isn't the first place you'd think a young designer would come from, but it was home for most of my life. I have been a designer since 2012, designing event posters and promotional material for the local council. Since my debut I've been designing for a range of clients including Twisted Plastic Records in conjunction with Ministry of Sound, FReeZA, Wyndham City Council, Corangamite Shire, Igaz Sound & Lighting, and Dark Tempo Warrnambool. Many of these clients broadened my range in services, pushing me to design logos, posters, tickets, brochures, business cards and much more.

Since the beginning of Maddox Designs, I have acquired a Certificate III in Media, a Diploma of Graphic Design, and I am currently studying for a Bachelor of Communication Design. Print Design and Motion Graphics are favourites of mine, although I am confident and willing to experiment with Web Design and Illustration. With a bit of technology and a lot of music, I am in my element and always excited to begin a new project.
